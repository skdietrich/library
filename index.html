The Author

Stephen Dietrich-Kolokouris, PhD., is a historian and researcher whose work navigates the complex intersections of technology, history, and the unexplained.

A former law enforcement officer, Dr. Dietrich-Kolokouris brings trained investigative discipline to his academic pursuits. His unique perspective influenced by his having lived abroad for many years. He earned a PhD. from Johann Wolfgang Goethe University in Frankfurt.

He currently focuses on how historical narratives and emerging technologies shape our modern understanding of existence and the supernatural.
